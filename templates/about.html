About Us

Serving for Good Health Pharma rose established with a team of young, dynamic & experienced people in the year 2010. We are always committed in make Life Stronger Together with medical fraternity, social organizations & the society. Our core commitment is towards improving health care and creating value for all our stakeholders including doctors, patients, regulators, employees and business partners. We are passionate and enthusiastic about our goal and highly optimistic about our future in view of the exciting opportunities of expansion and growth at different stages of development, promises to continue providing high quality medicines manufactured in WHO-GMP facilities with good R&D backup. The prime objective of the company is to become most admired company by doctors, patients, employees and all channel partners.
